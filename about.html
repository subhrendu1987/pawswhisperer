About Shaizad Ali Khan

Shaizad Ali Khan is a certified dog trainer with 15 years of experience. Over the years, Shaizad has successfully trained 20 dogs, transforming them into obedient and well-behaved companions. His dedication and passion for dog training have made him a trusted name in the industry.

Our mission is to help every dog live a happy and obedient life. Shaizad uses positive reinforcement techniques to ensure that training is both effective and enjoyable for dogs.

Certifications

  • Certified Professional Dog Trainer (CPDT)
  • Animal Behavior College Graduate
  • Member of the Association of Professional Dog Trainers (APDT)

Experience

With 15 years of hands-on experience, Shaizad has worked with a variety of breeds and temperaments. His approach is tailored to meet the unique needs of each dog and owner.
